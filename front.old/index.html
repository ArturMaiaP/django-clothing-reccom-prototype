A Conversational Recommender System to Improve User Experience in Clothing Fashion e-Commerce - Evaluation Experiment

Nowadays, improving the customer's shopping journey through meaningful and personallized interactions is a challenge for e-commerce. Trying to offer you a personalized shopping experience and to save you time, we developed a smart tool DigAI that interacts with you to learn your preferences based on your feedback. You are kindly requested to help us evaluate the tool DigAI. For that, we designed an evaluation experiment in which you try to select a skirt you like or would possibly buy from a fashion e-commerce site without and with the tool's assistance. The experiment should take you less than 15 minutes. More details on the experiment are given through the Instructions button below. Results will be kept anonymous. Thank you for your contribution.
