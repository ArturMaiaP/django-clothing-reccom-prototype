USER EXPERIENCE IN CLOTHING FASHION E-COMMERCE - EVALUATION EXPERIMENT

You are kindly requested to help us evaluate scenarios of Recommender Systems' applications to Fashion E-commerce. The experiment should take you less than 15 minutes. Results will be kept anonymous. Thank you for your contribution. Your goal is to find a striped mid-size skirt that you like and would "buy".
